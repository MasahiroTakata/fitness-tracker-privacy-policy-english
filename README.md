The application “Health Household Ledger” (hereinafter referred to as the “App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This App does **not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The App does **not** collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

User data stored on the device is used solely for providing the App’s features, such as recording, displaying, and analyzing information.

## 3. Use of Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use any third-party services such as advertising networks, analytics tools, or external APIs.

## 4. Data Management

All data is stored locally on the user’s device, and the user has full control over it.
If the App is uninstalled, all locally stored data is deleted from the device.
